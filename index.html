Figure 1. Decomposition of gross modelled removal x̂ = 100% under the current Isometric uncertainty-deduction approach (C = x̂ − σe − σm − LCA) and NPSP option (c) (C = (x̂ − σe) · 1[x̂ > x̅] + k − LCA). The dashed line marks threshold x̅. Under option (c), σm is managed through the threshold gate rather than as a per-tonne haircut; once the threshold is cleared, measurement uncertainty does not reduce the marginal credit rate. Lump-sum k = 5% (illustrative) compensates fixed entry costs independently of removal volume. Baseline values α = 8%, β = 10%, LCA = 4% are consistent with the Tufts Cove uncertainty budget (Isometric 2025b).
